About Megazz Services

Our journey from a small home-based business to a community leader

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Megazz Services began its journey as a humble home-based car wash, expanding its offerings to include plates of Braai for its customers. The inception by Beatrice and Benny Mathew marked the beginning of a venture that would grow into an all-purpose business aimed at serving the Postdene community in Peymansburg, Northern Cape.

The challenges of the Covid-19 pandemic, particularly Benny's retrenchment, catalyzed the business's diversification and growth, leading to its current status as a full-scale kitchen and service provider.

Beatrice & Benny Mathew - Founders

Our Vision

At Megazz Services, our vision is to become a beacon of excellence and innovation within our community and beyond. We aspire to enrich lives through a diverse range of high-quality services, fostering a vibrant, self-sustaining community. Our commitment extends to creating employment opportunities, nurturing talent, and contributing to the economic and social vitality of Postdene.

Our Mission

Megazz Services is dedicated to providing exceptional, diverse services that meet the evolving needs of our community. Our mission is to deliver unparalleled value through our comprehensive range of offerings, from culinary delights and bespoke carpentry to meticulous cleaning services and beyond. We are committed to excellence, innovation, and the continuous development of our operations.
